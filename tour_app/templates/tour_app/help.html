    • What is Europe Tours?

    How Does Europe Tours Work?

    Europe Tours showcases thousands of tours running all around the world by over 2500 operators. TourRadar is a one-stop-shop to search and compare tours to help you find your dream tour with the perfect operator for you using our amazing search functions and tour detail pages. Once you’ve chosen your dream tour, you can book your preferred departure date directly from the website! Depending on which operator you’ve booked with, you’ll either receive your confirmation from one of our helpful Customer Service Travel Experts or from the operator directly. You can then continue to communicate with us or the operator by sending any messages from your Booking Conversation Page. You can also request additional nights of accommodation and airport transfers, add in your personal details, and make any modifications to your booking from your Booking Conversation Page!

    How to Use the Search

    Searching for tours on TourRadar is easy! Simply use the search bar on the homepage to type in a region, country, city, or tour operator and select the month you’re looking to travel if you have one in mind, or leave this blank if you don’t. You can then use the filters on the search results page to narrow down the results to find the tour that’s just right for you! If you already have a specific tour in mind, you can simply type the tour name into the search bar to bring up that tour!

    How do I Find Out How Much Each Tour Costs?

    You’ll see the advertised price on the tour overview and as the main price on each tour details page. This price is the 'FROM' price, meaning that the most affordable departure date is this listed price. Each departure date can be different, as pricing can vary depending on seasonality, availability, applicable sales, etc. so you’ll then need to check each departure date from the tour details page to see an accurate price for the date you’re hoping to book. To do so, click 'Check Availability' to bring up each departure date and its price
